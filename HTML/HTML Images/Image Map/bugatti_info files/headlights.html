Bugatti Headlights Info

  • Flat full-LED projector: Bugatti's headlights are the flattest full-LED projectors in the automotive industry.
  • Carbon fiber light trims: Bugatti uses carbon fiber for the light trims of their headlights.
  • LED daytime running lights: Bugatti headlights have LED daytime running lights.
  • Three-dimensional surface: Some Bugatti headlights have a three-dimensional surface to reduce aerodynamic drag.
  • Airflow management: Bugatti headlights have features that manage airflow to cool the engine and improve performance.
