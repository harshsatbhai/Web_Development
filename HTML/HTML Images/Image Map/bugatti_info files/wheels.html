Bugatti Wheels Info

Design

  • Bugatti wheels are made from aluminum
  • The wheels have a signature eight-spoke design
  • The wheels are lightweight and can withstand high speeds and forces
  • The wheels can be painted different colors

Materials

  • Made of cast aluminium featuring eight flat and wide spokes, a removable wheel rim and an integrated brake drum
  • Bugatti continues to research and develop new materials to make the wheels lighter and more aerodynamic

Performance

  • Bugatti wheels are designed to transfer forces to the wheel bolts and provide stability and safety
  • The wheels are subjected to high forces and repeatedly checked for cracks
